Laura Kerrivan: 2021 ESPLAR Elite Series Champion

Before 2021, Laura Kerrivan was considered to be the "Greatest Legacy of Failure" in ESPLAR Motorsports. Racing in the top division of the Evanshire Super Pro League of Auto Racing from 2010 until 2021, Kerrivan notched 18 wins, 29 top 5s and 68 top 10 finishes. Her championship runs never seen to have ended well no matter how much she tried, as Mylo-Kerrivan Motorsports, the team her father Ben Kerrivan co-owned until 2022, was often cursed with bad cars, technical issues, and wrecks not of her own doing. Now, after winning her only championship in 2021, Kerrivan will go down as among the last of the old guard of the 2010s era, with ESPLAR increasingly dominated by younger drivers.

Timeline

  • Born November 6, 1991 in Lakeview, Arcadia
  • Begins racing in 1999
  • First race victory: Rockport 2004 (Late Model series)
  • Dominates the 2009 ESPLAR regional championship to earn a full time ride for 2010
  • First EES Victory: New York 2010
  • Has always remained with Mylo-Kerrivan Motorsports from 2010 until 2021
  • Comes up short in the 2014 EES Championship, loses the 2016 EES championship by 14 points
  • The ultra competitive 2018 EES season would be her worst yet, though she would still salvage a good points spot at the end
  • Is eliminated in the 2019 EES playoffs
  • Finally wins the 2021 ESPLAR title, then retires out of the series immediately following the season
